# Senior citizen — advance tax and regime-switching

**Applies to:** Senior Citizen (60+) profile
**Last verified:** PENDING — not yet populated against a current source.

## What this covers
The usual senior-citizen advance-tax exemption, and how it — and the
free year-to-year regime-switching normally available to a pure pension/
capital-gains taxpayer — both change once any business or speculative
(intraday) income is present. A senior citizen with speculative income
can only move from the new regime back to the old regime and back again
once in their lifetime, not freely every year.
