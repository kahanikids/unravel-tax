# New Income Tax Act 2025 — transition notes

**Applies to:** every profile, every year during the transition
**Last verified:** PENDING — not yet populated against a current source.

## What this covers
The new Income Tax Act 2025 applies from FY2026-27 onward; filing for
FY2025-26 (AY2026-27) — the current filing season as of this repo's
initialisation — is still governed by the old Income-tax Act, 1961. Some
mechanics move on the calendar's clock rather than the income-year's
clock during the transition (e.g. Form 15CA/15CB renamed to Form 145/146
from 1 April 2026, regardless of which year's income is being reported).
This distinction — "the form name follows today's date, the tax
treatment follows the year the income was earned" — is easy to get wrong
and worth stating explicitly wherever it applies.
