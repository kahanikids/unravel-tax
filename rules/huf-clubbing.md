# HUF clubbing (Section 64(2))

**Applies to:** HUF profile
**Last verified:** PENDING — not yet populated against a current source.

## What this covers
If a member transfers a personal asset into the HUF without adequate
payment, income from that asset is clubbed back to the transferring
member's own return — not taxed in the HUF's hands, even though the HUF
holds the asset. Distinct from, and more stringent than, the general
spousal clubbing rule, and a commonly missed rule when families
consolidate assets into an HUF structure.
