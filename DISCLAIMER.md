# Disclaimer

Unravel Tax helps you organize Indian income tax filing data and applies
documented rules mechanically. It is **not** professional tax, legal, or
accounting advice.

## Not government-affiliated

Unravel Tax is an independent open source project. It is **not** affiliated
with, endorsed by, or connected to the Income Tax Department of India, the
Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT), the Ministry of Finance, or any other
government entity.

Always verify figures and filing requirements on the official portal:
[incometax.gov.in](https://www.incometax.gov.in).

## What this tool does and does not do

- **Does:** Read your documents (locally in your browser), organize numbers,
  flag gaps and common risk triggers, and export a CA summary plus a detailed
  workbook.
- **Does not:** Submit your return, sign forms on your behalf, or replace
  judgment on ambiguous situations.

Every output should be reviewed by a qualified Chartered Accountant (CA)
before you file, especially for NRI, HUF, single-parent clubbing, or any year
with a rate change or an unclear transaction.

## Estimates only

Calculations follow versioned rule files in this repository (`rules/*.json`).
They may contain errors, lag behind a Budget or Finance Act amendment, or not
cover your exact situation. Treat all numbers as **estimates** until your CA
confirms them.
